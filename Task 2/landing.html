About Me

I’m Gunjesh Kumar Gunjan, a B.Tech student specializing in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata (graduating 2026). I’m currently refining my portfolio to showcase interactive features, real-time audio processing, and recruiter-friendly tools. My goal is to create technology that’s not just functional—but impactful.
